Preventable conditions have some treatment, therapy, or lifestyle change that can be made to help prevent or avoid symptoms. Non-preventable conditions have no known interventions that will help to prevent them.

Cystic Fibrosis is an example of a condition that is not preventable. While there are treatments that may help reduce symptoms, there is no medical intervention that will completely prevent the disease from occurring.

Other diseases in this category include osteopetrosis, immunodeficiency syndromes, and certain cancers
